Hello Bagel!

Bagels are known for their chewy crust and soft interior. Traditionally, the dough is first boiled and then baked, which creates their distinctive texture. Montreal Style

Montreal-style bagels contain malt and sugar with no salt. They are boiled in honey-sweetened water and baked in a wood-fired oven.

St. Louis Style

St. Louis-style bagels are defined by their slicing method. They are sliced vertically into thin pieces instead of one horizontal cut.

Other Bagel Styles

Other variations include Chicago-style steamed bagels, San Francisco-style flatter bagels, and traditional London bagels.
